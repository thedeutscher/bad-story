title: Interlude - Riding the Waves
---

The story jumps back to the past and follows Zoey, then still the protagonist's girlfriend, and what happened with her up until having returned at the end of episode 8.

The protagonist and Zoey hang out in her home and discuss music and school. She goes downstairs to bring a beer as her mother tells her something is wrong with her grandmother and that she's going to visit her. Zoey shows concern for grandma and asks her mom to let her know that she loves her. Back upstairs with the protagonist, they have sex and it is revealed that Zoey has been trying to get the protagonist to talk more with her in bed but he has been reluctant of doing so and especially after she laughed at him last time. It is apparent that Zoey and the protagonist have trouble discussing their emotions with each other.

Zoey's mom is back with news that grandma has passed away. Zoey is devastated and flashes back to her memories of grandma, who was always on her side and believed in her aspirations despite her stepfather's objections. As she's grieving, she decides that in honor of grandma she has to pursue her dream of becoming a surfer, using the inheritance grandma left her with. She prepares to leave for a surfing school in San Diego. The protagonist escorts her to the train station and they part ways. On the way to California, Zoey reflects on her relationship with the protagonist and reads her diary, which contains some sketches and thoughts. It is revealed that what made her laugh at the protagonist is that she was proud of him after he managed to ask her for something sexual.

In San Diego, Zoey checks in to a hostel which forces her to wear a wristband indicating that she isn't of legal drinking age, to Zoey's dismay. At the surfing school Zoey partners up with Emma, a heavily tatooed and pierced local adult woman who sees the sketches in her diary and takes her to the tatoo shop she works at as a tatoo artist. At the shop, Zoey meets the shop owner Bret and another tatoo artist named Jonah. Emma convinces them to hire Zoey as an assistant to the shop.

Over the next few months, Zoey grows anxious with her mediocre performance at surfing. She becomes better friends with the people at the shop, especially Bret, as her relationship with the protagonist weakens. Zoey gets her first tatoo, a chest tatoo of wings in memory of grandma. It is revealed that Jonah is gay and that Bret and Emma used to be a couple before Emma moved to Vegas to pursue her dreams and their relationship weakened, until finally becoming only friends when she came back to San Diego. Zoey eventually stops talking with the protagonist altogether, noting that it's probably for the best, similar to what Bret told her Emma did when she left for Vegas which helped him get over their breakup.

Emma and Zoey do a topless photo shoot of each other, as Emma sends a topless photo to the new employee at the tatoo shop Jenna, who replies with her own topless photo and it is revealed that she's lesbian. Zoey gets her nipples pierced by Emma as Bret accidentally walks in on them and is uncomfortable with having seen Zoey naked. Zoey and Bret do some more bonding and Emma grows suspicious of them, eventually accusing Zoey of having sex with Bret. A few days later, in a burst of anger, Emma posts the topless photo of Zoey on social media as revenge for supposedly having sex with Bret. Zoey enters Emma's apartment and they fight, and as Zoey tries to calm Emma down and convince her she didn't do anything with Bret, Emma breaks to tears as she confesses she's in love with Bret but have never told him, having assumed that he didn't pursue her more aggressively when she left for Vegas because he's not interested in her. Zoey tells her Bret's side of the story and that she should talk with him about her feelings.

One evening at the shop, everyone is very drunk as Emma confesses her feelings to Bret and they make up. Zoey and Jonah leave the place as Emma suggests Zoey to join them for a threesome, but Zoey declines and leaves. Bret and Emma have sex as a half asleep Jenna cheers them on.

Zoey starts running out of money and eventually realizes that she doesn't like surfing, and sees in what happened between Bret and Emma a reflection of her and the protagonist, a relationship they both wanted that dwindled because they were unable to talk about their true feelings with each other. She says goodbye to her friends at the shop and packs her stuff to get back home. After reuniting with her mother, she goes to the protagonist's house to see what's up with him. Neil opens the door, and they smile and hug.

Back at the present, the protagonist is in the bathroom at the Burke house, in shock of what just happened, as Neil calls. He tells Neil that this isn't a good time as Neil tells him Zoey returned and wants to talk with him, adding further trouble to his already complicated situation. He tells Neil to give Zoey his new number as Sage knocks aggressively on the bathroom door and calls him out, saying that they need to talk.

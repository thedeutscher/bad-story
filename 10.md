A flashback shows the life of Maya's and Derek's parents. Their mother Helen is sick with arthritis and is having difficulty performing her daily tasks, barely even being able to hold her newborn twins. At dinner, she teaches Patrick how to say his prayers, as she prays that she'll live to see her children getting married. As the disease progresses, her physical and mental health deteriorate, and by the time they are young children she's in a wheelchair. The next day at work in the car dealership, Patrick is at a loss and looks to the cross, praying that he will be able to fulfill his wife's wish, as the scene jumps to the bitter-looking Patrick of the present.

Back in the present the protagonist wakes up and goes to clean the DIK mansion together with the HOTs and the other DIKs.

In Sage's room, the protagonist, Maya, Josy, Sage, Derek and Jill meet to reveal Maya's situation to Jill so that she may provide legal help. Jill is unable to help because Maya doesn't have the contract of her loan, and Jill suggests going to Isabella for help. The group meets Isabella at the library and let her in on the situation. Isabella doesn't know what to do but then Derek and Maya come up with a plan to steal the contracts from Patrick's room when they go back home for the Thanksgiving dinner. Josy, being reminded of the "beard" concept that she told the protagonist about after he told her about Chad's secret, suggests Maya bringing the protagonist home to help with the operation by pretending he is her boyfriend, and Sage volunteers to also come with them by posing as Derek's girlfriend.

Before they leave the library, Bella reveals to the protagonist that she knows he cheated in midterms but won't report it, and that he has to stop cheating from now on because she is taking a risk by hiding it. As he leaves, Jill catches him for a talk and reveals she saw on one of the DIK posters being spread the image that him and Derek took in Tybalt's room during hell week, now realizing that they are the burglars Tybalt told her broke in and stole her underwear. The protagonist confirms that they really did break into her room but denies having stolen her underwear, claiming that it is Tybalt who stole them, as Jill doesn't know who to trust on this and walks away disappointed.

The DIKs meet in the mansion to discuss the posters being spread, as people start to freak out. They blame Tommy for hanging the pictures up in a place that party guests have access to, and reveal that the preps crashes the Halloween party, blaming the protagonist for letting them in. The protagonist chills everybody down and they agree to tear down any posters they see while trying to investigate which of the party guests is responsible.

The next day, everybody takes their yearbook photos. Ashley is mad at Anthony for bullying the nerds. Derek asks to talk with Ashley in private sometime later, intending to tell her about the camera in his room. Lily tells Maya that Sage asked her to investigate what kind of party Maya would want to celebrate her joining the HOTs, and specifically if it should be HOTs only or include the DIKs. Maya says she is fine with a mixed party.

Zoey is working at a beauty clinic and chats with Jacob and Jonah. The protagonist meets Nora at the gym who asks him out on a date. As he trains in the punching bag, the gym personnel who helped him last time reveals that his name is Dwayne and he is a personal trainer and offers the protagonist to join their fight club for no extra cost. Tommy hangs the photo of Leon's losing the CUM-petition on the wall and instructs him to go plan Thanksgiving with the protagonist. They invite Derek and Jamie and Jamie tells them that the tradition is that the new maggots show the appreciation to their elder DIKs by organizing a dinner for them. They decide to organize a barbecue in a park near campus the evening before the actual Thanksgiving dinner. Derek almost burns the kitchen by trying to demonstrate what are flaming shots. Lily hears about the barbecue plan and they decide to combine Maya's rejoining celebration and the DIK Thanksgiving.
